# User Interviews

# Objective

The purpose of these interviews was to understand how users manage AI tooling costs and identify common pain points.

---

# Interview 1

## Participant
Computer engineering student using ChatGPT, Cursor, and GitHub Copilot.

## Pain Points
- Multiple AI subscriptions
- Difficult to track monthly spending
- No centralized dashboard

## Key Insight
Users want visibility into all AI-related expenses.

---

# Interview 2

## Participant
Freelance web developer.

## Pain Points
- Paying for overlapping AI tools
- Uncertain which subscriptions are actually useful

## Key Insight
Users want optimization recommendations before renewing subscriptions.

---

# Interview 3

## Participant
Startup enthusiast and SaaS learner.

## Pain Points
- AI tools becoming expensive over time
- Lack of spending analytics

## Key Insight
Users are interested in benchmark comparisons with other teams.

---

# Common Themes

Main recurring problems:
- subscription overlap
- increasing monthly AI spend
- lack of optimization guidance
- no centralized tracking platform

---

# Product Decisions Influenced

Based on interview feedback, the project includes:
- audit workflow
- savings estimation
- recommendation dashboard
- benchmark insights
- spend analytics

---

# Conclusion

The interviews validated that AI tooling costs are becoming a real concern for developers, startups, and technical users.
